Who We Are

Welcome to Moldovan Mnt. Mickey Mouse, the most magical place where the towering peaks of Moldova’s mountains meet the big, bright smile of Mickey Mouse! Think of it as a wild, fun-filled rollercoaster ride through nature, sprinkled with a whole lot of Disney magic. We’re the ultimate mash-up of Moldova’s breathtaking scenery and the cheeky charm of Mickey Mouse – where every corner is packed with fun, laughter, and surprises for families, kids, and even grown-ups who are young at heart.

Our Story

It all started with a lightbulb moment—why not create a place where Moldova’s rich culture meets the timeless wonder of Mickey Mouse? The dream was simple: to build a spot where you can soak up the beauty of the mountains, giggle like a kid again, and enjoy the perfect combo of adventure and tradition. So, we took a picturesque corner of Moldova, threw in some enchanted Mickey magic, and voila! Moldovan Mnt. Mickey Mouse was born. Imagine a place where Mickey's joyful spirit frolics through lush forests, where mountain trails are lined with laughter, and where you can explore, play, and have a ball. From heart-pumping rides to quiet walks under the stars, it’s the kind of place that’ll make your inner child dance with joy. Come for the adventure, stay for the magic!/p>
