Services

1. Fresh Dairy Products

At Moldovan Mnt. Mickey Mouse Dairy Farm, we produce a wide range of delicious dairy products made from the finest milk. All of our products are crafted with care and are free from artificial preservatives, ensuring the best taste and nutrition.

  • Fresh Milk: Whole, skim, and organic options
  • Cheese: A variety of soft, semi-soft, and hard cheeses
  • Butter: Rich, creamy, and made with the highest quality cream
  • Yogurt: Creamy, smooth, and packed with probiotics
  • Ice Cream: Made in various flavors, perfect for the whole family

2. Farm Tours & Experiences

Get an up-close look at how our farm operates! Our guided farm tours give you the opportunity to explore the beauty of our farm while learning about sustainable farming practices, animal care, and the dairy production process.

  • Meet the Animals: Get to know our cows, goats, and chickens
  • Behind-the-scenes: See where we milk our cows and make our dairy products
  • Picnic Areas: Relax in our scenic picnic areas and enjoy a delicious lunch with fresh farm products
  • Interactive Experience: Milk a cow, feed the animals, and try your hand at making cheese
  • Free Cup of Milk?
